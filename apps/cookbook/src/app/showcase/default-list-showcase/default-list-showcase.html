Default List adds styling to every <ul> and <ol> in Kirby. It adds a margin of 8px to the left of the entire <ul> or <ol>, and a margin of 8px to the right of every individual bullet or number in the <li> element(s).

  • Two
  • Three
    1. 3.1
    2. 3.2
    3. 3.3
