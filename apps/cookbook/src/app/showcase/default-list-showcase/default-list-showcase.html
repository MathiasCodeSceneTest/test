Default List adds styling to every <ul> and <ol> in Kirby. It adds a padding of 4px to the left and a margin of 16px to the bottom of the entire <ul> or <ol>, and a padding of 12px to the right of every individual bullet or number in the <li> element(s). Lastly it adds a margin of 8px to the top of every <li>, except for the first <li> element within the <ul> or <ol>.
